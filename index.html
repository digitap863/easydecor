cornice partition

A Cornice is a horizontal decorative molding that crowns around the top of an interior wall. It fits in the transition between the wall and the ceiling, hides cracks and imperfections and adds dynamism to the interior ceiling design giving a sleek, modern and royal look to your space.Rose Domes or Ceiling Rose are placed in the center of the ceiling to add a grand look and enhance the aesthetics of the space. Chandeliers, fans, lights, etc are then placed in the center of Rose Domes to immediately upgrade the interiors.
